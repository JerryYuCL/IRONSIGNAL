Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

IRONSIGNAL values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information: such as your name, email address, and shipping address when you create an account or place an order.
  • Usage data: such as pages visited, device and browser type, and IP address.
  • Cookies: to improve your browsing experience and website functionality (see our Cookie Policy).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide products and services to you.
  • To communicate updates, offers, and news (with your consent).
  • To improve our website, products, and customer service.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • We never sell your personal data.
  • We may share data with trusted partners for order fulfillment, payment processing, or legal compliance.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

  • You may opt out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in our communications.
  • You may update or delete your account by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email us at contact@ironsignal.co.
