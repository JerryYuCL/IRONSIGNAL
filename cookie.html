Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025

IRONSIGNAL uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and to better understand how our website is used.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They help websites function, remember your preferences, and gather analytics.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for basic site functionality.
  • Performance cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site.
  • Functionality cookies: Remember your preferences and choices.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services (such as analytics providers) that set their own cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

  • You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings.
  • Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.

5. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

6. Contact

For questions about our use of cookies, please email contact@ironsignal.co.
